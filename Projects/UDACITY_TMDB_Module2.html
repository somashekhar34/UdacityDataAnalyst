Introduction¶

This is the dataset of tmdb movies here we can find the several factors which effect the revenue,type of the movie should be found out.Some of the features are id,imdb_id,popularity etc.¶

Questions that are to be posed on this dataset and answered in the future are:¶

  • Find the number of movies of the all the directors where the revenue is more than or equal to three times the budget
  • Find all the movies of the director named "Colin Trevorrow" which have Action genre?
  • Find out the imdb_id of the movie which has the highest votecount ?
  • what were the most popular genres in 2000 compared to 2015?
  • Do movies with higher vote count recieved better ratings?
  • which movie had made the highest profit?
  • which cast person has appeared the most?
  • what are the most popular genres year by year?
  • what is the average runtime of all movies?
  • which director directed most films?
  • Find all the titles of the movies released in 2015 which have more popularity than the middle value of popularity of all the films?
  • what are the ids of all the movies which where produced by the production company "UniversalStudios"?
